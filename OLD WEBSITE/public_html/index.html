OUR PROCESS

The UpForge 8–Step Flagship Process
Built For Brands That Are Ready To Move
STEP 1
Business & Founder Context Alignment
Understand founder vision, constraints, and what "winning" looks like.
STEP 2
Market & Competitive Landscape Analysis
Scan trends, threats, saturation, and whitespace.
STEP 3
Customer Intelligence Deep Dive
Extract true pain points through direct user insight.
STEP 4
Strategic Wedge & MVP Offering Design
Lock the use-case, design the leanest testable offer.
STEP 5
GTM Channels & Message Testing
Launch across 1–2 focused channels, test narrative and positioning.
STEP 6
Pilot, Pricing & Iteration Loop
Run lean pilots, validate pricing, track usage, optimize fast.
STEP 7
Scale Playbook & Ops Engine
Codify what works, build systems, hire or automate..
STEP 8
Ongoing Performance & Growth Governance
Monitor KPIs, embed feedback loops, and refine the roadmap for sustainable growth.
